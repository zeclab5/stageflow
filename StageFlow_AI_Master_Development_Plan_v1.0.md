# StageFlow AI Master Development Plan v1.0

## 비전

공연 기획부터 공연 종료까지 모든 과정을 AI가 지원하는 Stage Production
OS 구축.

1.  Prompt Studio
2.  Project Manager
3.  Storyboard AI
4.  Asset Manager
5.  Video Workflow
6.  Resolume Assistant
7.  Projection Mapping AI
8.  Lighting AI
9.  AI Stage Director
10. Live Performance AI
